Anaheim Convention Center is the Largest LEED Certified Center in the USA

The Anaheim Convention Center (ACC) is the largest of the 14 LEED Certified convention centers in the US. The ACC was awarded the Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED) certification by the U.S. Green Building Council (USGBC). LEED is the USGBC’s leading rating system for designing, constructing and operating the world’s greenest, most energy efficient, and high performing buildings. The ACC's initiatives included aligning efforts to reflect LEED criteria, an intensive recycling program, efficient and water-saving plumbing fixtures and an expanded energy-saving electricity and solar energy system. Not only is the ACC green, but Anaheim as a destination is as well. Disney's One-Of-A-Kind Spectacular Attracts Group Business

Groups can elevate their next event experience at the Disneyland Resort to magical proportions with “World of Color,” a new nighttime spectacular at Disney California Adventure theme park. “World of Color”  is a 25-minute visual symphony that takes attendees on a fascinating journey of Disney stories, both charming and adventurous in a whole new way.  Created with groups in mind, the show offers a variety of private viewing locations that can accommodate everything from small executive groups to opening/closing galas of up to 6,000 attendees.  Buy-outs are also available, which enables event organizers to customize the show with organization logos and other exciting features.
